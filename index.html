My Travel Story

        

Hi! I'm Maliha Shahrin, a passionate traveler who loves exploring nature and beautiful coastal spots. I've visited some amazing places like the Butterfly Garden in Ames, Iowa, and the picturesque beaches of Laguna Beach, Santa Monica, and Long Beach. On this website, I share my travel experiences, tips, and beautiful photos of the places I’ve been.

        

My goal is to inspire others to explore the natural beauty of the world and make unforgettable memories.

Best Time to Visit the Butterfly Garden

        

The best time to visit the Butterfly Garden in Ames is during the spring and summer months when the flowers are in full bloom and the butterflies are most active.

        

Must-See Beaches in California

        
                
  • Laguna Beach: Great for art and relaxation.
    •             
  • Santa Monica: Famous for its pier and vibrant boardwalk.
    •             
  • Long Beach: Ideal for both city life and scenic coastal views.
